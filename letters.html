Poems for You

Always You
I still remember that Gwalior day, I looked down from my room, and there you lay. Not inside anything called a bed, not asleep, just out on the street, Nervous standing there, so so sweet. You glanced up, and I swear I froze. That smile, that face, that perfect pose. My heart said, "There he is... the one," And in that moment, love had begun. You looked so cute that I swear I just stared; Without wasting time, I came down... my soul scared. That world turned light the moment I saw you, As if every wrong had just gone right.
The Light We Loved
In our small Jaipur Airbnb room The astronaut lamp would gently bloom Its golden glow so soft and bright Wrapped us both in quiet light Outside the world would rush and race But inside time would slow its pace Just you and me in that soft hue A moment still just made for two We didn’t talk, we didn’t need Your eyes said all my heart could read You looked at me and in your gaze I saw love in a hundred ways Now when I miss your hand in mine I turn it on and let it shine Not just a lamp, not just the night But you and me and that little light
Every word, a piece of my soul 💗
